KONGU ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Kongu Engineering College is an autonomous engineering college located at Perundurai,Erode district in the state of Tamil Nadu in India. It is affiliated to Anna University and accredited 'A' Grade by National Assessment and Accreditation Council. Kongu Engineering College was ranked 135 among engineering colleges by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in 2020. Campus placement training (HR, Technical, GD, Written Test) is given in the pre-final year for all eligible candidates. Kongu ENgineering College has recently signed an MoU with CoreEL Technologies that helps conduct various training activities.

    GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

    The participants selected for presentation at "CYNOSURE'21 " are requested to adhere to the following instructions:

  • Two members per team.
  • Paper should be in any one of the following topics:
    1. Robotics & Image Processing
    2. Communication & Security
    3. Big data analytics and cloud computing
    4. Advanced trends in IT
  • Presentation Specification:
    1. Font Style: Times New Roman
    2. Line Spacing: 1.5
    3. Font Size: 14 points for heading & 12 points for contents
  • One soft copy of the abstract should be submitted on or before 15/02/2021.
  • The maximum time for presentation is 10 minutes.
  • Selected papers will be intimated on 18/02/2021.
  • Full paper submission should be submitted on or before 21/02/2021.
